Online Registration

Registering for online services in APEX FTB , it's easy and only takes few minutes.

During the registration process, a unique account number (userID/username) will be assign to you for logging into your account.

Once you have completed the registration process your account will be activated. After account activation, you will receive an activation notification within the period of 1-2 business days to the email provided during registration. Upon receipt of the new account activation notification, you will be able to log into your newly activated account.
